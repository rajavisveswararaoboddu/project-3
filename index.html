Kedarkantha Trek

A mesmerizing winter trek in Uttarakhand known for its scenic views and snow trails.

Special Attractions:

  • Snow-covered trails
  • Summit sunrise view
  • Camping under starlit skies

Why Visit?

One of the best treks for beginners and adventure lovers with breathtaking summit views.

Price & Duration:

Price: ₹10,000 | Duration: 6 Days

Eligibility:

Suitable for beginners and experienced trekkers, aged 10+.

Health & Safety Measures

  • 💧 Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of water to prevent dehydration.
  • 🥾 Proper Footwear: Wear sturdy trekking shoes to avoid injuries.
  • 🩹 First Aid Kit: Carry essential medical supplies for emergencies.
  • 🌞 Sun Protection: Apply sunscreen and wear sunglasses to protect from UV rays.
  • 🥗 Nutritious Food: Eat energy-boosting snacks and maintain a balanced diet.
  • 🗺️ Navigation Tools: Carry a map, compass, or GPS to avoid getting lost.
  • 👕 Layered Clothing: Dress in layers to adjust to changing weather conditions.
  • ⛺ Safe Camping: Choose a secure spot and follow local safety guidelines.
